SuperB

SuperB is a native iOS app that help basketball players to train their dribble skill. We started to develop SuperB after our research and interview. We found that dribbling skill is the most basic and crucial skill in basketball. It is also one of skill that can be trained alone at home. But we found a few pain points in training dribbling skill alone which are lack of drills set menu, lack of training progress, and lack of feedback. We want to help tackle those problems with SuperB. We also want make the drills more interactive by simulating a real drills used by coach to distract players by pocking their off hands with a stick while they’re dribbling to train their concentration, ball handling, and reaction time. For the tech , we use Vision Framework to detect body pose and to detect basketball object for dribble counting using datasets that we gather together.
In this project my role is Lead Developer and mainly working on the Vision part.

1. Master your drills start from the basic to advance
2. Give feedback based on your result
3. Track your progress

Tech Used : Swift (UIKit), Vision , CreateML, Core Data

Dreamer

A Team project made in Apple Developer Academy. We start from a big idea "Finance" and we want to make someone manage their money better. After our research we found that having and making a S.M.A.R.T goal is essential to help someone manage their money better. That's why we made Dreamer , A native iOS App for first time worker to manage money better by creating attainable goal ("pay yourself first" method).
1. Suggestion based on goal and income
2. Information about goals
3. Count how much you need to save by also considering goals and inflation

Tech Used : Swift (UIKit), Core Data
